Hi, I'm Felix Song

I was born in Ridgefield Connecticut, but I've grown up mostly in Newton Massachusettes, where my family and I currently reside. As a child, I was always curious about nature and science, influenced by my parents who both aquired PhDs in STEM fields.

Throughout my K-12 education, I've always been interested in pursuing a career in STEM. Not only do I beleive that understanding the natural laws of the universe give one insight in almost all walks of life, but to simplify the complexities of life was fun. As a kid, we take a lot for granted and we don't bother to understand many phenomena. Speeding up as you sled down a hill, why your arm gets red around a scratch or why hot chocolate feels so good in the winter snow. All of the these random happening can be explained Through science and as a grew older, I felt more and more inclined to know the "why" to everything.

Outside of academics, I have several hobbies that I have given me meaningful experiences that help me connect to others and share persepctive. I loved atheltics as a kid and still to this day as I feel its just as important to maintain good health as it is to grow our knowledge to fulfill our dreams and aspirations.
