My Senior Thesis

My senior thesis explored novel approaches in unfolding detector effects from high energy particle collisions. The approach utilized a conditional denoising diffusion probabilistic model, a generative AI trained to remove potential mis-information from technical bias of the detector, to reconstruct data from simulated particle collisions. This approach is then comapred to common unfolding methods, specifically bin-by-bin, matrix, and Singular Value Decomposition (SVD) methods.

This project not only honed my technical research skills but deepened my appreciation for the whole process of creating a thesis questions, researching potential solutions and previous material, developing such solutions for our project, analyzing results, and writing a comprehensive thesis.
